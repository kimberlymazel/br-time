OUR BEST SELLERS

BRGERS

Meat Lover Burger

Patty made from the laziest black cattle on the island of Kyushu. The flavour of the fat is exquisite, with a buttery, tender texture that dissolves in one's mouth. Paired with dijon mustard, pickles brined in Champagne Ardenne vinegar and the freshest organic produce - BRGER's finest creation.

Free-range Chicken Burger

A juicy poultry patty that's pakced with flavours thanks to Greek yoghurt dressing, Parmesan cheese, five spice rub and fresh parsley, on a freshly baked bun. Grilled a few minutes on a hot skillet that makes them golden and crisp on the outside, yet tender and juicy inside.

SIDES

Animal-style fries

Fresh, hand-cut, French-imported Russet potatoes prepared in 100% sunflower oil, dressed in 100-day aged cheddar and slices of Wagyu beef that has been marinated in authentic bulgogi sauce.

Onion rings

Buttermilk onion rings twice flash-fried to maintain sweetness of onion and its cripsness, tossed in a secret blend of spice. Wonderfully paired with any choice of BRGERs.

DESSERT

Molten Lava cake

Made with the highest quality chocolate from the Venezuelan region of Chuano, the official supplier to the Kings Louis XVIII, Charles X and Louis-Philippe. The ooey-gooey center is whipped and folded by hand and is infused with vanilla pods and Italian truffle oil.

Animal-style Banana split

Caramelized banana splits with the chocolate drizzle and three scoops of the finest gelato; Gelato alla Fragola (strawberry), French vanilla bean, and chocolate with Venezuelan cacao nibs. Perfect encore to our classic beef BRGER.
