About us

How it all started

BRGERS is an Indonesian restaurant founded in 2021 by Alysha Maulidina (2502005906) and Kimberly Mazel (2502022250) that is centered around serving high quality burgers for high quality customers. Our headquarters reside in Jakarta, Indonesia, where we first started BRGERS. We wanted to create a place where customers can trust what happens in the kitchen, where we use both local and imported ingredients to give the healthiest option, yet remain true to our origin taste. We aim to have complete trust and transparency with our customers and are honoured to receive the trust and respect that we get today.
